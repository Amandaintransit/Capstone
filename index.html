Kentucky Child Support Estimator

A tool to help you understand what information the Commonwealth of Kentucky considers when establishing child support obligations and to give you an idea of what you might be entitled to or what you might owe when the formula is appplied to your particular circumstances. The governing statutory scheme can be found in Kentucky Revised Statutes (KRS) Chapter 403.




This tool was designed to provide an estimated child support oblgation according to Kentucky law; however, its accuracy depends on the information provided and there may be additional circumstances that present a court with discretion to vary. The tool is designed to be informative but it is not a guarantee.
